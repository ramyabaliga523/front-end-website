AMAZING TEAM WORK WITH PROFESSIONAL PHOTOGRAPHER

Sherman Mcclain and Rene Thomas founded Raw Vision in 2004. We've had the privilege of photographing some of the most incredible people in collaboration with photographers Jasper Knox and Zadie Manning. Our reputation has grown by word of mouth, and we are becoming more well-known with each challenge. We've worked with modelling agencies, event organizers, performers, schools, individual models, and private events like weddings and parties along our journey.
